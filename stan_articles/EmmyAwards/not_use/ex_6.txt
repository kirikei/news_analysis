Actor Matthew McConaughey (above) and his True Detective co-star Woody Harrelson have received Emmy nominations for Actor in a Drama Series. Their roles in the gritty crime drama have drawn widespread acclaim. Picture: Richard Shotwell
New York - Here is a list of nominees in key categories for the Emmy Awards, the highest honours in US television, announced on Thursday.
The Emmys are awarded by the Academy of Television Arts and Sciences in a ceremony that will be broadcast on August 25, from the Nokia Theatre in Los Angeles, on the NBC television network.
