BBC News - Cilla Black 'disappointed' over Harris
TV presenter Cilla Black has said she is "disappointed" after Rolf Harris was found guilty of 12 counts of indecently assaulting four girls in the 1960s, 1970s and 1980s.
She said: "I've never known Rolf be any different to anyone else. I thought he was lovely."
Black and Harris both performed on the 1960s variety show A Swingin' Time.
