Until recently, smoking in restaurants, bars, coffee shops smaller than 100 square meters was exempt but no longer. The fine for owners of establishments will be up to $1,500. The smoking ban policy went into effect in 2012 and now the government will start enforcing the policy. Want to smoke? Go look for a nearby designated smoking room or area.
