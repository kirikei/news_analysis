Gazette Staff 4:25 p.m. EDT April 3, 2015
CIRCLEVILLE – Two search warrants in Pickaway County resulted in the seizure of gaming machines, cash and documents Friday.
According to the Pickaway County Sheriff’s Office, the warrants were served at Spinners Casino on U.S. 23 in Circleville and a residence in the 14200 block of Walnut Creek Pike, Ashville, belonging to Spinners owner Stephen Cline. A total of 143 gaming machines were seized.
The warrants were served following an investigation initiated by numerous complaints that Spinners was operating slot machines that paid out cash prizes in violation of Ohio law. The investigation is ongoing.
