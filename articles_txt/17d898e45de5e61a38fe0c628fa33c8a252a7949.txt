Crude-oil futures are marginally higher in early Asian trade Wednesday
By
Updated May 26, 2015 10:20 p.m. ET
0216 GMT [Dow Jones] Crude-oil futures are marginally higher in early Asian trade Wednesday, attempting a rebound after sharp overnight losses of nearly 3% on the back of a strong U.S. dollar. In the last trading session, Brent crude probed the lowest level since April 22 as the U.S. dollar index climbed to the highest level in a month on stronger than expected U.S. economic data, Citi Futures says. “At least for the time being, the strength in the U.S. dollar has called attention away from declining U.S. petroleum inventories,” analyst Tim Evans says.
