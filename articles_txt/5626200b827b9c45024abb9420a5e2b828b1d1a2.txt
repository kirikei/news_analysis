HELENA, Mont. (AP) — Family, friends and many of Montana's state and federal leaders attended the funeral service for former state Gov. Tim Babcock .
KFBB-TV reports ( http://bit.ly/1CGpQsi ) that former Gov. Marc Racicot spoke about his fond memories that he spent with Babcock.
Racicot says Babcock was a humble man who never forgot where he was from.
