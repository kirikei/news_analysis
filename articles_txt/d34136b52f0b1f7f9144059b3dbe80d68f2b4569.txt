Ex-defensive end McGinest latest inductee into Patriots Hall of Fame BY MARK FARINELLA  SUN CHRONICLE STAFF The Sun Chronicle | 0Â comments
FOXBORO - Willie McGinest said Tuesday that he never knew anything but high expectations for himself as a member of the New England Patriots.
"We went to the Super Bowl in my second year with the Patriots, and I knew that Robert Kraft had a lot of expectations to grow the franchise and make it one of the best, premier franchises in the league," the former defensive end said. "After that first Super Bowl in '96, I knew that we had the opportunity to do something great. The expectations for me and for the organization were always set high."
