By Michael Macagnone
Law360, Washington (April 10, 2015,  7:51 PM ET) -- The U.S. Department of Defense revealed its Better Buying Power 3.0 procurement best practices initiative Thursday, aiming to put greater emphasis on cybersecurity and maintain the country’s technological edge.
Deputy Defense Secretary Robert Work said Thursday that the DOD is in danger of falling behind emerging technological threats and, in a time of tight budgets, needs to place a higher priority on keeping ahead of the competition. The DOD is consequently looking to reduce barriers to getting commercial technological breakthroughs into the defense sector.
