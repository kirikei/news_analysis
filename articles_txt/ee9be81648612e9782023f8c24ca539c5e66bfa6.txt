Cheney book to attack Obama on foreign policy
President Obama and Dick Cheney.
(Photo: Associated Presss)
One of President Obama's most vociferous critics -- ex-Vice President Dick Cheney -- is taking his case to the printed page.
"Exceptional: Why the World Needs a Powerful America," to be published Sept. 1, will focus on Cheney's long-held view that Obama has abandoned the U.S. tradition of global leadership, according to the publisher Threshold Editions.
The George W. Bush vice president, who is writing the book with daughter Lynn Cheney, will argue that Obama has "significantly diminished" American power at a time of growing threats, the publisher said.
