PHILADELPHIA (CBS) — Mayoral candidate and former Philadelphia District Attorney Lynne Abraham collapsed during a debate Tuesday night.
The Democratic mayoral candidates were going through the first round of questioning when Abraham passed out on stage.
She fell over for a few minutes and the other candidates came to her aid.
She was taken off stage to receive medical attention.
A spokesperson for Abraham tells CBS 3 Eyewitness News Abraham is okay now and recovering.
Lynne Abraham campaign:  It was simply a matter of her not eating throughout the course of the day in combination with the hot lights. 1/3
Lynne Abraham campaign: Doctors have assessed her heath and deemed her 100% okay. 2/3
Lynne Abraham campaign: Lynne is a tough cookie and we expect to continue fighting for the people of Philadelphia in this campaign. 3/3
The debate is being held at the Kimmel Center and broadcast live on local television.
