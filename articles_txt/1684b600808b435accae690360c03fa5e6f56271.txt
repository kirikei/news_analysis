Keira Knightley Gives Birth, Welcomes First Child With Husband James Righton
Keira Knightley and her musician husband James Righton have welcomed their first child together, multiple sources confirm to Us Weekly .
That same month, Knightley, 30, joked about going into labor during an appearance on The Ellen DeGeneres Show.
"My plan is to get it out," she said, laughing. "[I'll] drop it out, keep working. Pick [the baby] up, that's my plan. That would be wonderful for television, but not so good for me."
Knightley and Righton, 31, went public with their relationship in April 2011. They wed in May 2013 in a small France ceremony. E! News was first to break the news that Knightley had given birth.
