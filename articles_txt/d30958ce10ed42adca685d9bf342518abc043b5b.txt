Rand Paul, seeking presidency, walks tightrope of his father's legacy
RAND PAUL: Son must emerge from dad's shadow yet hold political base
LOUISVILLE, KY - APRIL 7:  Sen. Rand Paul (R-KY) delivers remarks...
AUSTIN - In one of his first moves as an official presidential candidate, Rand Paul last Thursday used a World War II ship to combat attacks describing him as an isolationist unwilling to go after terrorists. Speaking in front of South Carolina's USS Yorktown, the libertarian-leaning senator said if he wins he would "do everything it takes to defend America from these haters of mankind."
