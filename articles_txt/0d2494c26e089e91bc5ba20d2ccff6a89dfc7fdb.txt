Lincoln's assassination chair still fascinates 150 years later
The upholstered seat rocking chair in which President Abraham Lincoln was assassinated on April 14, 1865.  The chair  was originally located in the president's special box in Ford's Theatre, Washington, D.C. sits on display on Friday April 10, 2015 at the Henry Ford Museum in Dearborn.
