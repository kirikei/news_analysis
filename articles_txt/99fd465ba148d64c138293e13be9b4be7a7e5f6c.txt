COLORADO SPRINGS, Colo. (AP) — Voters in Colorado Springs are choosing a new mayor.
Six candidates are vying to replace incumbent Steve Bach in a mail election that ends Tuesday. The field includes former Attorney General John Suthers and former Colorado Springs mayor Mary Lou Makepeace.
The Gazette (http://bit.ly/1H1vcoy ) reports that Suthers outraised all of his opponents by more than a 3-to-1 margin.
