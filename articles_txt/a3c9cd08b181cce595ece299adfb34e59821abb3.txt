Presidential candidate and Kentucky Senator Rand Paul releases his third book, "Taking a Stand: Moving Beyond Partisan Politics to Unite."
In it, he describes his history planting trees at his home in Bowling Green writing, "I'm a crunchy conservative and a tree hugger and proud of it."
In the book, he calls the Republican Party's image "broken" saying the brand "sucks."
The Republican is trying to expand his base by appealing to environmentalists and minorities which traditionally tend to vote Democratic.
