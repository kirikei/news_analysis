The state legislature passed bills to expand the places where a concealed carry permit holder can have their gun.
The legislation would allow permit holders to bring their guns to concerts, rest stops,  public parks and political rallies.
Opponents said they wouldn't feel comfortable at those locations knowing someone could have a weapon.
The bill will now go to the governor.
