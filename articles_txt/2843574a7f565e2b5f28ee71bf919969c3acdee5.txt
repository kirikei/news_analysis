GOP renews push to repeal in-state tuition law
Legislators want to do away with letting undocumented immigrant college students pay same as resident
AUSTIN - For the third time in as many legislative sessions, Republican lawmakers  on Monday renewed their push to repeal a long-established state law that allows many undocumented immigrant students to pay in-state tuition costs at public Texas colleges.
