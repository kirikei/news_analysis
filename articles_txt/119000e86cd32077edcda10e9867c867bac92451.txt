CUSTER - Walkers from the Southern Hills will gather at 6 p.m. on Friday, June 5, at the Custer High School Track for the Relay against Cancer.
The 2015 Relay For Life of Southern Hills features participants as they walk relay-style around the track and take part in fun activities off the track.
Teams can include coworkers, club members, family and friends who have gathered donations prior to the event.
The event activities include a survivor reception at 4:30 p.m., the opening ceremony at 6 p.m., themed laps, silent auction, face painting, Fight Back Ceremony at 9 p.m., and food will be available from Custer High School Cheer team.
For more information on how to participate go to www.relayforlife.org/southernhillssd.
