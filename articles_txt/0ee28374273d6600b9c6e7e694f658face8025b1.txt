John Rollins: Rollins readies for home game at Byron Nelson
John Rollins makes his way back to Irving, Texas, for this week's AT&T Byron Nelson Championship.
The Texas resident is very familiar with the area and will be making his 14th consecutive start at the event, thanks to a sponsor exemption. He's finished top 30 in seven of those previous 13, but we've seen an out-of-form Rollins over the past two seasons.
