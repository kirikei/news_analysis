May 26, 2015
The Red Sox and Twins are in a rain delay at Target Field in Minneapolis as they attempt to  get in the second game of their three-game series. Twins officials have said they are hoping to start at 8:30 p.m. local time, which is 9:30 p.m. Eastern time.
Here are the lineups:
TV/Radio: NESN / WEEI.
Red Sox vs. Pelfrey: Ramirez 10-47, Sandoval 4-17, Ortiz 0-2, Pedroia 2-3.
Twins vs. Buchholz: Hunter 5-29, Mauer 7-16, Plouffe 1-13, Suzuki 8-15, Dozier 2-12, Hicks 2-8, Escobar 3-6, Nunez 1-2.
Stat of the Day: The Red Sox have not won three straight since April 9-11.
Left turn: David Ortiz is 6 for 56 (.107) against lefthanders. Ortiz hit .293 against lefties from 2011-14. His last home run against a lefty was on Sept. 4, 2014 when he hit two against Chris Capuano. Overall, Ortiz is 4 of his last 27 and hitless in his last 15 at-bats.
On Target: Dustin Pedroia is a .344 career hitter (73 of 212) against the Twins including 24 of 64 (.375) at Target Field.
Other notes: Buchholz has allowed six earned runs over 21.2 innings in his last three starts. The Red Sox are 2-7 in Buchholz’s starts. He is 3-1, 4.25 in eight career starts against the Twins. … Pelfrey faced the Red Sox once in his career, in 2009 when he was with the Mets. He allowed two runs over seven innings. … Mike Napoli is 11 of his last 25 with six extra-base hits and 10 RBIs. … Daniel Nava is 4 of 38 (.105) in his last 16 games. … The Twins are 25-12 since a 1-6 start. They are 16-6 in May.
Song of the Day: “Get Buck in Here” by DJ Felli Fel.
