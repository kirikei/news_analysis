Ferguson Set for First Elections in Wake of Unrest
Half of municipal seats up for grabs in poll that could shift city council’s racial makeup
By
Updated April 6, 2015 3:36 p.m. ET
Voters in Ferguson, Mo., head to the polls Tuesday for the first municipal election since a fatal police shooting last year led to weeks of unrest in this St. Louis suburb and exposed deep racial imbalances in local law enforcement, courts and government.
