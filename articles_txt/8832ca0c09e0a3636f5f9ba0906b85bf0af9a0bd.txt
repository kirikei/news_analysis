Rahm Emanuel Re-Elected as Chicago Mayor in Runoff
Incumbent defeats challenger Jesus ‘Chuy’ Garcia after failing to win a second term outright in February
By
CHICAGO—Mayor Rahm Emanuel was re-elected Tuesday following a spirited runoff here that tested the policies and personality of the nationally known politician.
Preliminary results showed Mr. Emanuel leading 55.5% to 44.5% over challenger Jesus “Chuy” Garcia with 94% of precincts reporting.
