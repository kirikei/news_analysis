By Associated Press April 9 at 6:33 AM
RICHMOND, Va. — Capitol Police say someone stole two signs commemorating the Civil War’s sesquicentennial that were posted in Capitol Square.
Capitol Police say in a news release that the thefts occurred on April 4 during the “Future of Richmond’s Past” event. The National Park Service signs display enlarged antique photo reproductions.
Police are searching for a male suspect who was captured on surveillance video. Police say the man was accompanied by a woman and a dog.
