Sloane Stephens has streak broken by Maria Kirilenko | The Times
Sloane Stephens and Sam Stosur were the highest-profile casualties yesterday in the women's draw as the majority of the seeds navigated their way through to the second round.
After reaching the second week in her previous six grand-slam tournaments, Stephens fell at the first hurdle against Maria Kirilenko, the Russian who is ranked 109th in the world. The 21-year-old American, seeded No 18, lost the first set 6-2 but saved five match points in the second before eventually losing in a tie-break.
