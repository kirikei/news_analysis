Tiger Woods rues error-strewn round that ends hope of Open recovery | The Times
Tiger Woods lamented a host of errors that saw him tumble down the Open leaderboard as he continued to struggle to find his form following back surgery.
The three-time Open champion recorded a 73 on the third day, for a three-over total, in only his fifth competitive round since an operation in March.
Woods opened with successive birdies, having begun at the par-five 10th due to the R&A's decision to hold a two-tee start because of the threat of bad weather.
