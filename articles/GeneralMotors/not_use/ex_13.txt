GM clarifies that Cadillac 2014 CTS sedan not part of Monday's recall | Detroit Free Press | freep.com
General Motors said today that one of six recalls issued did not include all of the 2014 Cadillac CTS models, only the 264 coupe and wagon body styles that were built before the redesigned CTS sedan was launched.
The 2014 CTS sedan was incorrectlty listed among the six recalls issued Monday covering 8.4 million GM vehicles in North America.

The company has recalled nearly 29 million vehicles worldwide this year amid a safety crisis that has spawned government investigations, lawsuits and a settlement fund for victims of a separate ignition switch problem that was ignored for more than a decade.
