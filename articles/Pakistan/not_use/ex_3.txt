U.S. Drone Strike Kills Five Militants in Pakistan's North Waziristan - WSJ
PESHAWAR, Pakistan--A U.S. drone strike in Pakistan's North Waziristan tribal region killed at least five militants of the Afghan Haqqani Network on Wednesday, intelligence officials said, the second such strike on the militant group this month.
